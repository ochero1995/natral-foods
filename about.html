About Us

Natral Foods was founded in March 2015 by Jamie Richardson and Anderson Cooper with the aim of helping the hundreds of thousands of people across the country to manage their food intolarnces without comprimising on quality or price.

Our stock is widely sourced from over half a dozen specialist producers that make everything from gluten free pasta to chocolate brownie mix and rice cakes. Natral Foods has partnered with DPD to support our delivery infrastructure, this allows us to offer a one day delivery service on all of our products.

Jamie Richardson

Jamie has been a student at University of Geneva for two years and has almost two years of experience in the Health Nutrition sector working at Wickham Bishops Health Foods.

Anderson Cooper

Anderson is currently studying Computer Science at University of Geneva with the aim of learning the ins and outs of the business world in the hopes of bringing this knowledge to Natral Foods.
